Leaf's Awakening

Broken Umbrella Leaf Blade is pre-hardmode sword. It can be crafted after defeating any Evil Boss.
You can view it source code here
It was added in version 0.2 of the mod. It is upgrade to Leaf Blade
In version 0.3 its damage got decreased (50 -> 25) and its scale increased (1 -> 3.25)
In version 0.35 its sell price got changed to 1 gold 50 silver, and crafting station got changed from workbenches to anvils

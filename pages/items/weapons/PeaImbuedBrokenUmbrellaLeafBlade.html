Leaf's Awakening

Pea Imbued Broken Umbrella Leaf Blade is pre-hardmode sword. It can be crafted after defeating any Evil Boss.
You can view it source code here
It deals less damage than Broken Umbrella Leaf Blade but it shoots homing projectiles: Peas, these are the same ones that Pea Shooter shoots.
It was added in version 0.3 of the mod. It is upgrade to Broken Umbrella Leaf Blade
In version 0.41 its sell price got changed to 2 gold 50 silver, crafting no longer requires Umbrella and Rich Mahogany Leaf Wand and crafting station got changed from workbenches to anvils

Damage Type: Leaf Damage
Base Damage: 7
Knockback: 6
Use Time: 20
Use Style: Swing
Sell Price: 2 gold 50 silver
Rarity: Green
Auto Reuseable?: Yes
